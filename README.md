# Hospital-management-system
Description:A Hospital Management System (HMS) is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline and automate the day-to-day operations of a hospital. It helps manage clinical, administrative, and financial tasks efficiently, ensuring smooth coordination across all departments. The system provides secure access to patient records, appointment scheduling, doctor management, billing, pharmacy, laboratory reports, and staff activities.

The HMS reduces paperwork, minimizes errors, improves patient care, and enhances decision-making through real-time data and reports. By integrating all hospital processes into a single digital platform, the system ensures faster service, improved workflow, better communication, and increased operational efficiency.
Objectives:
1. To streamline hospital operations by automating key processes such as patient registration, appointments, billing, and report generation.
2. To improve patient care and safety by ensuring accurate, up-to-date, and easily accessible medical records.
3. To enhance coordination between doctors, nurses, administrative staff, pharmacy, and laboratory departments.
4. To reduce paperwork and human errors by shifting to a digital and automated workflow.
5. To speed up decision-making through real-time data, analytics, and reporting tools
6. To maintain secure, confidential, and role-based access to patient and hospital data
7. To improve resource utilization including beds, equipment, staff, and inventory.
8. To provide a seamless patient experience from admission to discharge with efficient service management.
9. To integrate all hospital functions into a single platform for better transparency and communication.
10. To support administrative and financial efficiency through automated billing, inventory tracking, and records management.
